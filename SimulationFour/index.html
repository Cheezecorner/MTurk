Welcome

In the next screen, you will be presented with two panels, side by side.
Each panel will contain a random assortment of 40 to 200 blue and 20 to 100 orange marbles for 1 to 5 seconds.
After you finish observing each panel, you will be asked
  1. How many orange marbles you thought there were in each panel, and
  2. How confident you were about your guess.
A higher confidence yields a higher reward, awarded only if the actual number of marbles lands within a narrower range.
You will be rewarded only for one of the two panels after you complete both!
